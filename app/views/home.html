Personality Type

We will go now through a personality type test. It's of utmost importance that you answer all the questions honestly, and not try to give answers that would depict how you would like to be. The objective is to understand yourself as you really are, not how others expect you to behave. Every personality have ups and downs, none of them define succes in life, what's really important is to know yourself.

As the test starts, you will be shown a series of statements for which you will have to choose between "Yes" or "No". In some of them you might feel it's not an absolute yes or no, just choose the option that comes closer to how you feel about it.

Please, bare in mind that the test consists of 72 questions, so a little time and patience is adviced.
