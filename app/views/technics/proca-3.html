Step Two
Divide in Subtasks

Let's suppose you have to do a project for next week, so you add "Make project" to your list of tasks. At first sight there's nothing wrong with this, however you have to be careful, for this is a mistake that encourages procrastination. You'd be surprised how many tasks we abandon or put off just because we treat them as if they were indivisible.

Break down large tasks into subtasks until you can do one of them in a single day.
