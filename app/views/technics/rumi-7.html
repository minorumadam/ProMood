Step Five
Learn from your mistakes

The quickest way to find success is to fail over and over again.

For example, I was once 30 minutes late for an interview. I did not get the job and I became very self-critical of my tardiness. Once I asked myself "what is the lesson I learned?" I quickly calmed down and applied this lesson to future experiences.

I now leave my house one hour early for interviews, which has served as a valuable lesson. No need to continue to berate myself. In addition, frequently remind yourself how far you've come. Every time you make a mistake, you learn something new.
