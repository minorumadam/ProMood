Step Three
Don't Overload

Having a long list of pending tasks will make you feel helpless. Be realistic and set a number of tasks that you know you are able to fulfill. If you don't accomplish a task one day, it's not serious, but if it becomes a habit it can generate frustration, stress and negative thoughts that play against your productivity.
