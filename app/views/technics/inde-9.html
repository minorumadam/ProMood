Step Nine
Final Recomendations

Don't be afraid to say yes or no. Act according to what you think and feel, but make a decision. This assertive attitude will lead to an improvement in your personal development.

Forget about indecision, doubt and fear.

Act according to what you really think and feel, if you don't want to do something don't do it. If you don't want to say yes to a proposal that doen not suit you, don't do it, remember that nobody is forcing you to do it.

Be firm, but consistent. If you know that some action will harm you or goes against your values or personal interests, don't do it, take the right decision.

Neither be impulsive, doing so leads to mistakes and failure in several aspects of life, and this is worse than indecision.

Analize your behavior in relation to the decisions you make, so you can achieve a better life quality by taking assertive decisions that will benefit you both personally and socially.
