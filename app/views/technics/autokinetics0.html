Introduction

Autokinetics is the practice of automatic or spentaneous body movements that produce vital energy. It is based on three simple steps, and contrary to what happens with aerobics, yoga and other therapeutic programs, autokinetics require no willpower, discipline or endurance.

Some people are reluctant to try anything new because they remember the effort that had to be made in other programs, but autokinetics involves no work, it's an energetic stop, a recess time in which we can practice some simple and fun movements.

All you have to do is make a pause for about ten minutes, allowing the body to move at its own will, without seeking any purpose, without work and effort, it's a break from the demands of everyday life.

Be prepared to reload your energy.
