You have just taken your first step into the world of conscious well-being.

For most of the people the mood is something that just occurs, something over which we have no control...

Nothing could be further from the truth.

Our moods are malleable and you can learn how to change, improve and enhance them consciously.

We created proMood for this great purpose.

But for the app to be really useful we need to know more about your personality, that's why this first time we will ask you for a little patience to perform a personality type test. With this information it will be easier for us to guide you through your mood states and find solutions more adequate to your personality.

You will be provided with advice and techniques to practice and improve your different moods. But remember, change and improvement comes only with work and commitment, it's vital that you take the time to perform this tasks a little bit every day. The best way to keep your daily work is through the notifications system, it's set by default twice a day but you can change it to accommodate your daily preference.

This is the heart of proMood, but there's a lot more! Find extra trainings, use our Chat feature, deal with panic attacks efficiently, and a much more.
