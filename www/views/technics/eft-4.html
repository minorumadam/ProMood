Step Three - Nine Ranges
Perform the following actions while you tap continuously on the 9 Ranges point:
  1. 1) Close your eyes
  2. 2) Open your eyes.
  3. 3) Turn your eyes strongly to the right and down (keep your head straight).
  4. 4) Turn your eyes strongly to the left and down (keep your head straight).
  5. 5) Spin your eyes circularly.
  6. 6) Spin your eyes circularly in the opposite direction.
  7. 7) Buzz 5 seconds of any song.
  8. 8) Count from 1 to 5.
  9. 9) Buzz 5 seconds of a song again.
