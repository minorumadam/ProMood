How to do it

The first step is to find the rhythm of the body. For this, you need to sit down and slowly start to swing your body until it reaches a natural and spontaneous motion.

The second step is to improvise movements, letting your body express through improvisation, from the smallest movements to the postures and gestures of dance, keeping yourself open to the possibility of making the sounds that want to come out from you.

The third step is to keep yourself in this alignment zone for at least five minutes, where you allow the free and spontaneous expression of your body.
